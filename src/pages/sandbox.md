## The Sandbox Philosophy ğŸ’­

### Embrace the Mess
The sandbox is intentionally messy. Not every idea needs to be polished before sharing it.

### Fail Fast, Learn Faster
Some of the best learning happens when things don't work. The sandbox is where we figure out what doesn't work so we can find what does.

### Show Your Work
Even unfinished work has value. Seeing the process can teach others (and future you) more than seeing the final product.
